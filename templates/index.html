About Core Measurement Limited

A Nigerian-owned oil and gas service company specializing in measurement, monitoring, inspection, calibration, and metering solutions for the upstream, midstream, and downstream sectors of the energy industry.

With over three decades of combined industry experience, we have established ourselves as a trusted provider of technical assurance, operational reliability, and production accountability for both local and international energy stakeholders.

Core Measurement also offers technical training programs covering meter operation, flow measurement, data management, and equipment maintenance — ensuring continuous skills development and compliance with global metrology and petroleum measurement standards.

Our Process

Our service process is designed to ensure accuracy, transparency, and traceability in every measurement and inspection activity. From initial evaluation to final certification, each stage is guided by international standards, technical integrity, and a commitment to operational excellence.

Project Evaluation & Planning

We begin by assessing client requirements, defining operational objectives, and identifying the most suitable metering and inspection solutions in line with ISO, API, and OIML standards.

Field Inspection & Data Acquisition

Our certified engineers and inspectors conduct on-site measurements, sampling, and verification at terminals, platforms, and pipelines — ensuring accuracy, safety, and regulatory compliance.

Calibration & Technical Validation

Using internationally traceable calibration standards, we perform meter proving, system verification, and performance checks to ensure reliable and traceable measurement results.

Reporting & Certification

We issue detailed, defensible reports and certifications backed by verified data, ensuring transparency, regulatory compliance, and confidence in all custody transfer and production monitoring operations.
