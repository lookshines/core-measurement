About Core Measurement Limited

A Nigerian-owned oil and gas service company specializing in measurement, monitoring, inspection, calibration, and metering solutions for the upstream, midstream, and downstream sectors of the energy industry.

With over three decades of combined industry experience, we have established ourselves as a trusted provider of technical assurance, operational reliability, and production accountability for both local and international energy stakeholders.

Core Measurement also offers technical training programs covering meter operation, flow measurement, data management, and equipment maintenance — ensuring continuous skills development and compliance with global metrology and petroleum measurement standards.

Why Choose Us

We are dedicated to delivering precision, transparency, and accountability in every service we provide. Our reputation as a trusted oil and gas service company is built on a foundation of technical integrity, operational excellence, and commitment to international metrology and petroleum measurement standards.

With a team of experienced engineers, technicians, and industry experts, we combine local knowledge with global expertise to deliver accurate and traceable results that support clients’ operational, commercial, and regulatory objectives.

We leverage advanced metering technologies, certified calibration standards, and strong technical partnerships in the United Kingdom and Europe to ensure measurement integrity, loss prevention, and production accountability across upstream, midstream, and downstream operations.

Whether it’s metering, calibration, inspection, or production monitoring, our clients choose Core Measurement because we deliver data they can trust — enabling efficiency, compliance, and confidence in every transaction.
